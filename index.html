About Me

Pokemon TCG Pocket Extractor

This is a data workflow project designed to extract detailed information about Pokémon TCG Pocket cards and organize it into a structured database. Primarily developed in Python, the project automates data collection, cleaning, and formatting, making card attributes easily accessible for analysis or integration into other applications. This workflow streamlines the management of Pokémon card data and showcases expertise in data engineering and automation within the collectibles domain.
